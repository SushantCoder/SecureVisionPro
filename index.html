FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does remote video monitoring work? +

Our system uses high-definition cameras installed at your property, connected to our 24/7 monitoring command center via encrypted internet connections. AI algorithms continuously analyze the footage for suspicious activity. When a potential threat is detected, our trained security analysts are immediately alerted and can take action — including activating audio warnings, contacting law enforcement, and notifying you — all within 30 seconds.

How much can I save compared to security guards? +

On average, businesses save 60-70% on security costs by switching from traditional guard services to our remote monitoring solution. A typical security guard costs $25-$40/hour, meaning a single guard for 24/7 coverage costs $18,000-$29,000/month. Our Professional plan covers the same area with superior technology for just $599/month.

Do you provide service in both Canada and Dubai? +

Yes! We have operations in major Canadian provinces (Ontario, BC, Alberta, Quebec, and more) and across the UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah). Our Dubai operations are fully compliant with local security regulations, and we provide Arabic-speaking support for our UAE clients.

What happens when an intrusion is detected? +

When our AI or monitoring team detects an unauthorized person or activity: (1) A live audio warning is broadcast through on-site speakers, (2) Local law enforcement or security patrol is dispatched immediately, (3) You receive a real-time push notification with video clip, and (4) A detailed incident report is generated and shared with you.

Can I view my cameras remotely? +

Absolutely. All our plans include our mobile app (iOS & Android) and web dashboard access. You can view live camera feeds, review recorded footage, generate reports, and manage alerts from anywhere in the world at any time.

Is there a long-term contract? +

No. We offer flexible month-to-month plans with no long-term commitments. We believe in earning your business through excellent service, not locking you into contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.
